How To Make Fasoolia Betha That's Better Than YO BABA'S!

Fasoolia Betha Stew

This dish is a tomato based stew made using white beans and lamb or beef, but can also be made vegetarian. Best served with rice; this dish will sooth any soul and make a magic carpet of your sheets!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • White Northern Beans
  • Lamb or Beef
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Beef Stock or Water
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Gas X
  • Your Appetite!

Steps

  1. Cook lamb ahead of time and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and simmer for a few minutes until aromatic.
  4. Add tomato paste and stir. Simmer until dark red.
  5. Turn heat to medium high. Add stock or water and bring to boil.
  6. Add beans and mix well. Allow to simmer for a 45 minutes to an hour.
  7. Add meat and salt 10 minutes before serving.
  8. Add fresh parsley immediately before serving.
  9. Taste and adjust seasonings.
  10. Simmer until aromatic and oil is visible on top of stew. Beans will be soft, but not broken.
  11. Serve in a separate bowl and add on top of rice.
  12. Enjoy!
